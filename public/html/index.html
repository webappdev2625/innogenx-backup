Our Process

Schedule Repair

  • Schedule Repair

    No more waiting - simply schedule your device repair at a time

  • Visit Store

    Visit store - Come on down to your nearest Genius store at the time scheduled by you.

  • Device Repair

    Device repair - Our certified technicians will inspect and repair your device to be fully functional

  • Collect & Pay

    Collect & Pay - Collect your device and pay- it's as easy as that.
Mail-In Repair

  • Package Your Device

  • We Receive Your Device

  • We Fix & Send You Back

    We start repair on the same day & ship it back to you as soon as possible & you receive tracking no.
